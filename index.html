About Us

Welcome to MoonTour

MoonTour, your ultimate gateway to exploring the beauty of Malang and its surrounding areas. We specialize in providing top-notch car rental services and organizing memorable open trips to the most sought-after tourist destinations. Our mission is to make your travel experience in Malang and its environs as enjoyable and hassle-free as possible.

From the comfort of our latest model vehicles to the thrill of discovering new places with our open trips, we cater to every aspect of your travel needs. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that every journey with MoonTour is not just a trip, but an unforgettable adventure.
